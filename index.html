03Research system

From scattered opportunities to a verifiable public route into research.

A directory becomes infrastructure only when inclusion, provenance and maintenance are explicit.

Situation
Research participation opportunities are distributed across institutions, use inconsistent terminology and frequently lack stable, machine-readable records.
Move
I defined inclusion rules, verification fields, provenance, licensing and public data requirements, then operate the catalogue and its technical workflow.
Result
Yourself to Science: a public research-participation system with FAIRsharing and Zenodo records and machine-readable interfaces.
Evidence boundary

Implementation is AI-assisted; I own requirements, verification, functional testing, deployment diagnosis and operations rather than claiming unaided software development.
