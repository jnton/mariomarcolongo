01 · Paid scientific verification · public communication at scale

Helping a large science-communication operation stay evidence-bound

My role: Scientific fact-checker, writer and website manager
  • Investigate biomedical and evolutionary claims in primary literature
  • Surface uncertainty, conflicts and weak sourcing before publication
  • Explain corrections to a non-specialist production team
  • Operate the WordPress, hosting, DNS, SSL and technical-SEO layer

04 · Operating style

Useful when the problem is strange, ambiguous or uncomfortable.

This is the credible signal for unconventional, high-autonomy employers: how I approach difficult work—not a theatrical personality label.

Contrarian, not careless

I challenge assumptions and consensus, but I keep the evidence trail visible and distinguish a strong hypothesis from a verified conclusion.

High ownership

When I see a useful system missing, I define the workflow, coordinate implementation, test it, deploy it and keep it operating.

Comfortable with unusual problems

My work has included adversarial AI behavior, personal genomic data, taboo research topics and messy provenance questions.

Direct and systems-oriented

I communicate plainly, decompose ambiguous problems and prefer outcomes that can be inspected over polished claims that cannot.
